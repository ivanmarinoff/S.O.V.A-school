SCHOOL OF PSYCHOLOGY

The School of Psychology was created by the SOVA Psychological Center for those, who want to know more about psychology, want to know the human psyche works, what and how if forms our personality, are interested in relationships between between people and different patterns of behavior and its formation. And many, many more interesting and curious facts related to our functioning as people and individuals.. Thank you.

Learn more

WHO IS "SCHOOL OF PSYCHOLOGY" FOR?

The course is suitable for amateurs, students or students (over 18 years old) who want to understand more about psychology AND the fundamental aspects of psychological science and gain knowledge about the most fundamental aspects of psychological science.
